What makes it work

Adaptive memory. Real senses. Real personality.

Aura isn't a chatbot in a textbox. It hears, sees, and remembers — and quietly refines itself to match how you actually like to talk.

Visual memory

Every saved fact captures the camera frame at that moment. Ask later — it recalls what it saw.

Always-on voice

Open mic with sensitivity control, or push-to-talk for noisy rooms. Real-time response in your character's voice.

Adaptive style

"Shorter please" — and it's shorter. Reply length, tone, and topics tune to you over time.

Long-term knowledge

Conversations are continuously cleaned and adapted into a personalized training corpus. Your aura gets smarter about you, not just busier.
